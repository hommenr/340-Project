RW Brand Restaurant

This website is used to manage the RW brand of restarants. RW has restaurants accross the continental United States. Its restaurants range from fast food level to higher tier. This website was designed to track the restaurants that are built and close, manage the items from the brand that the restaurants select to serve at their location, and keep track of employees that are hired.
